Frequently Asked Questions

What is SurfSMS?

SurfSMS is a SMS texting service that sends you the surf report directly from NOAA and the National Weather Service, two times a day (that's how often they update it).

How does it work?

Signup with your credit card on SurfSMS.com and recieve the surf report in your SMS messages twice daily.

What does a sample SurfSMS text message look like?

+PLUS...The Next Day Forecast

How much does it cost?

$5 a month. That's it for 120+ Real Time Surf Reports a month (2 x a day + the next day forecasts)

Why is the information by county only?

That's how NOAA delivers it. Other surf reports are not NOAA based.

What is the the LOLA report then?

LOLA is Surfline's proprietary model and based on where I surf, its not very accurate. That is why SurfSMS only delivers you surf reports directly from the National Weather Service's Oceanic department called NOAA.

How do I signup?

Signup on SurfSMS.com with an active credit card.

When will I recieve SurfSMS reports?

As soon as NOAA makes their next update, you will recieve it via SMS. Currently NOAA updates come in around 2-3 am and 2-3 pm daily, subject to change any time.

What if I want an additional location, or multiple locations?

All of these features are coming soon, but please send us a note, so we know you are out there and where you surf.

How do I cancel?

Send us a quick email at yo@surfsms.com letting us know you want to cancel. That's it!
